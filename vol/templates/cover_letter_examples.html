Cover Letter Writing Tips
Do's:
  • Address the hiring manager by name when possible
  • Show enthusiasm for the company and role
  • Use specific examples and metrics
  • Keep it concise (1 page maximum)
  • Proofread carefully
  • Customize for each application
Don'ts:
  • Don't repeat your resume verbatim
  • Don't use generic templates without customization
  • Don't focus on what you want from the company
  • Don't include irrelevant personal information
  • Don't use overly formal or outdated language
  • Don't forget to include a call to action
