Getting Started

Creating your first resume is easy! Follow these steps:

  1. Sign up for a free account
  2. Click "Create Resume" from your dashboard
  3. Choose a template that fits your style
  4. Fill in your personal information, experience, and skills
  5. Use our AI suggestions to improve your content
  6. Preview and download your professional resume

Pro tip: Start with our "Professional" template for a clean, ATS-friendly design.

Your resume should include these essential sections:

  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone, location
  • Professional Summary: Brief overview of your career goals
  • Work Experience: Relevant jobs with achievements and responsibilities
  • Education: Degrees, certifications, and relevant coursework
  • Skills: Technical and soft skills relevant to your target job
  • Additional Sections: Projects, volunteer work, languages (if relevant)

Choose a template based on your industry and experience level:

  • Professional: Traditional, conservative industries (finance, law, healthcare)
  • Modern: Creative industries (marketing, design, tech)
  • Minimal: Clean, simple design for any industry
  • Creative: Stand out in competitive creative fields

All our templates are ATS-optimized, so you can't go wrong!

Billing & Plans

Upgrading to Premium is simple:

  1. Go to your dashboard and click "Upgrade to Premium"
  2. Choose between Monthly ($19.99) or Yearly ($199.99) plans
  3. Select your preferred payment method (Stripe or PayPal)
  4. Complete the secure payment process
  5. Enjoy unlimited access to all premium features!

Note: You can cancel anytime from your account settings.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time:

  1. Go to your account settings
  2. Click on "Subscription Management"
  3. Select "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Confirm your cancellation

Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. You can reactivate anytime!

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all premium subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with our service:

  1. Contact our support team within 30 days of purchase
  2. Explain why you're not satisfied
  3. We'll process a full refund within 5-7 business days

Note: Refunds are processed to your original payment method.

Features & Tools

Our AI resume analysis uses advanced natural language processing to:

  • Analyze Content: Review your resume for clarity, impact, and relevance
  • ATS Optimization: Ensure your resume passes through Applicant Tracking Systems
  • Keyword Matching: Identify missing keywords for your target job
  • Suggest Improvements: Provide specific recommendations for better results
  • Score Your Resume: Give you a comprehensive score and detailed feedback

Simply upload your resume or use our built-in analysis tool for instant feedback!

We support multiple resume formats:

  • PDF: Professional format, maintains formatting across devices
  • Word (.docx): Editable format for further customization
  • Plain Text: Simple format for online applications

Recommendation: Use PDF for most applications as it maintains your formatting and looks professional.

Our AI-powered cover letter generator creates personalized cover letters:

  1. Go to "Cover Letter Generator" in your dashboard
  2. Enter the job title and company name
  3. Paste the job description (optional but recommended)
  4. Select your experience level and tone preference
  5. Click "Generate Cover Letter"
  6. Review and customize the generated content
  7. Save and download your professional cover letter

Premium Feature: Available with Premium subscription.
