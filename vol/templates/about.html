Our Story

ResumeBuilder Pro was born from a simple observation: creating a professional resume shouldn't be complicated or expensive.

In 2024, our founder was helping a friend update their resume for a career change. They spent hours researching templates, formatting, and best practices, only to realize that most tools were either too basic or too expensive.

That's when we decided to build something different - a platform that combines the power of AI with professional design, making it easy for anyone to create a standout resume that gets results.
